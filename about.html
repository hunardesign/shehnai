About Shehnai
Shehnai Matrimonials a long journey of 20+ years..!!!
Dr. Shruti Manoj Modi

Shehnai Matrimonials was established by Dr. Shruti Manoj Modi, a practicing Anesthesiologist in Ahmedabad. Initially "Shehnai" was a small-scale home-based social activity service, to help the prospective candidates of friends and family find a suitable match. Her hobby soon turned into a passion, as she came across many parents who put a lot of faith in her work.

Managing a gradually flourishing business along with her Anesthesia practice was a challenge in itself but the immense satisfaction and good will that she received from so many parents gave her the strength to overcome this challenge and take Shehnai to the level that it is there today.

Till now it has been a very great support by the Medical fraternity to upgrade Shehnai's platform by putting a lot of faith in Dr. Shruti’s work & keep sending a lot of references. It's a very long Journey of hard work & dedication to create this platform for highly Qualified candidates.

She started realizing a rapid change the market in recent decade due to the advancement of technology and people adapting well to them. In other words, simple hard work was not enough for further growth in Shehnai’s work.

There came an idea to merge biological intelligence with digital intelligence , investing her connections and experience with equally sound technological group to develop a larger platform for all highly qualified candidates searching a matrimonial match.

We are excited to introduce a modernized Shehnai with a brand new website and partnership with technology experts. We hope that combination of experience, technological expertise and an interactive website will definitely ease the process and help our existing as well as new members to find suitable matches.

If you are seeking a life partner for yourself or for some other person in your social circle, you can depend on Shehnai Matrimonials. We look forward with pride to assist you in locating your life partner . You have come to the right place. We assure you to satisfy by putting an end to your search !
